Welcome to FurzoForYou, A Care Center Built "For You"

FurzoForYou is a learning platform designed to help families, active pet parents, and communities understand dog care through clear reliable information in one place created by students at Victorious Kidss Educares.

Why this website matters

This website matters because many people don’t have clear, reliable information about how to care for dogs properly. We make important knowledge simple and accessible, helping people care for dogs responsibly and interact with them more safely.

Why dog welfare matters

Healthy dogs are safer, calmer, and happier. When communities understand dog needs, everyone benefits: dogs receive better care, children learn safe behaviour, and public spaces become safer.

Better awareness also reduces fear, prevents avoidable incidents, and encourages kindness with responsibility.
