Dog Behaviour

Body language tells you how safe a situation is. Read signs early to prevent fear and conflict.

Signal Comparison Table

SignalLikely meaningBest response
Loose body and soft eyesComfortSlow friendly approach
Tail tucked + ears backFear/anxietyPause and create space
Stiff posture + fixed stareHigh warningBack away calmly

Quick behaviour notes

  • Barking can signal alert, fear, or excitement.
  • Growling is often a warning, not a challenge.
  • Hiding can indicate stress or pain.
