Responsible Ownership

Dog ownership is a long-term promise of care, training, and public responsibility.

AreaMinimum effortResponsible approach
HealthOnly when sickPreventive checkups and boosters
TrainingOnly during problemsRegular reward-based practice
Public safetyOccasional controlLeash, waste cleanup, trigger awareness
Remember: ethical care includes senior years, illness, and behaviour support.
Responsible care protects dogs and communities.
