Responsible Ownership

Ownership is a long-term responsibility of care, safety, and ethical treatment.

Commitment comparison

AreaMinimumResponsible standard
ExerciseOccasional walkDaily planned activity + enrichment
TrainingOnly when issues startConsistent reward-based learning
HealthOnly when sickPreventive vet care + boosters

Social responsibility

  • Use leash where required.
  • Clean public waste.
  • Prevent roaming and accidental breeding.

Ethical care

No neglect, no abandonment, no violence. Senior care and medical support are part of ownership.

Good owners create safer public spaces.
