Health & Warning Signs

Notice early signs and act quickly to avoid severe emergencies.

Emergency alerts

  • Breathing trouble
  • Collapse or seizure
  • Heavy bleeding
  • Sudden severe pain

Daily monitoring

  • Appetite and thirst
  • Energy level and movement
  • Stool and urination changes
  • Behaviour shifts
