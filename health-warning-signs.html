Health & Warning Signs

The earlier you notice changes, the easier treatment usually becomes.

Urgent warning signs: breathing trouble, collapse, seizure, major bleeding, repeated vomiting with weakness.

What to monitor every day

AreaNormalConcerning
AppetiteStable intakeRefusal for 24h+
BreathingCalm at restFast/open-mouth at rest
MovementNormal gaitLimping or stiffness

Behaviour changes can be health changes. Irritability, hiding, confusion, or sudden clinginess may indicate pain or illness.

If something feels unusual and persistent, call a veterinarian early instead of waiting for severe symptoms.

Observe daily, act early.
