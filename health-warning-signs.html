Health & Warning Signs

Early action saves lives. Watch both body symptoms and behaviour shifts.

⚠Emergency signs

  • Breathing distress
  • Collapse or seizure
  • Severe bleeding
  • Bloat symptoms

Illness indicators

  • Vomiting, diarrhea, appetite loss
  • Lethargy or unusual sleeping
  • Excessive drinking or urination changes
  • Pain posture or limping

AppetiteStable daily intakeSudden refusal >24h
