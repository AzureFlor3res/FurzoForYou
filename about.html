About FurzoForYou

FurzoForYou is an educational project that helps communities care for dogs responsibly and safely.

Mission

Make dog-care guidance clear, practical, and trusted for everyone.

Impact

We promote safer public interaction, better preventive healthcare, and humane treatment.

Core pillars

  • Behaviour awareness
  • Preventive health
  • Vaccination importance
  • Emergency readiness
Thank you for supporting responsible education.
