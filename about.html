About FurzoForYou

FurzoForYou is a public education project focused on safer, kinder, and more responsible dog care.

Our mission is to simplify important dog-care knowledge into guidance people can use immediately. We focus on prevention, awareness, and humane treatment.

We believe communities become safer when people understand dog behaviour, recognize warning signs early, and respond calmly in emergencies.
