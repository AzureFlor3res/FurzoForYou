About FurzoForYou

FurzoForYou is a public education project focused on safer, kinder, and more responsible dog care.

Our mission is to simplify important dog-care knowledge into guidance people can use immediately. We focus on prevention, awareness, and humane treatment.

We believe communities become safer when people understand dog behaviour, recognize warning signs early, and respond calmly in emergencies.

Our mission

Our mission is to spread awareness about responsible dog care and safe interaction with dogs. Through this community project, we aim to help people make informed decisions that protect both dogs and people.

Purpose of this website

This website was created as the main action of our community project to provide clear, organised, and reliable dog-care information. We designed it to make learning simple and practical so people can apply it in everyday situations.

Dog welfare awareness goal

Our goal is to improve understanding of dog behaviour, health, and humane treatment. By increasing awareness, we hope to prevent harm, reduce fear, and support better care for both owned and stray dogs.

Commitment to responsible education

We are committed to sharing accurate and responsible information about vaccination, safety, and ethical treatment. Our project encourages informed choices that support long-term animal welfare and community safety.

Community impact message

Small informed actions can create real change. When people understand dogs better and act responsibly, communities become safer, kinder, and more respectful for everyone.
