Daily Care

A healthy routine includes nutrition, movement, hygiene, enrichment, and comfort.

Morning: fresh water, meal, walk, toilet break.
Afternoon: rest + short play + mental enrichment.
Evening: training practice + grooming check + calm bonding.

Practical Questions

How much exercise is enough?

Depends on age and breed. Most dogs need daily walks plus play and sniff-based enrichment.

Why is sleep important?

Poor sleep increases stress and can worsen behaviour problems.

Routine care creates long-term health.
