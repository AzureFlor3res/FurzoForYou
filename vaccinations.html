Vaccinations

Vaccination protects individual dogs and also protects people through lower disease spread.

Why vaccines matter

Vaccines prepare the immune system before serious infection happens. This lowers disease severity and supports safer communities.

Protection against severe disease
Community protection effect
Risk when skipped

Core preventable diseases

  • Rabies
  • Parvovirus
  • Distemper
  • Adenovirus
  • Leptospirosis (region-based)
Action: Keep vaccine records and booster reminders in your phone and on paper.
Vaccination is prevention and responsibility.
