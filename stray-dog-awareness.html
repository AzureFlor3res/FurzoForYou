Stray Dog Awareness

Reduce conflict by staying calm, giving space, and using humane choices.

Risk chart

Running near dogs (high risk)
Crowding around food areas
Calm distance and slow movement

Never do this

  • Don’t throw objects.
  • Don’t corner dogs.
  • Don’t provoke barking groups.
If approached: stop and stand sideways.
Next: avoid eye contact and back away slowly.
If unsafe: contact local animal authorities.
Safe behaviour + humane treatment = better outcomes.
