Stray Dog Awareness

Safety around strays starts with calm behavior and respectful distance.

Stray dogs may be defensive because of hunger, injury, territory pressure, or fear. Sudden movement and crowding can increase risk quickly.

Do not run, shout, throw objects, or corner a stray. Stand still, avoid eye contact, and back away slowly when safe.

Step 1: Pause and keep space.
Step 2: Keep children close and calm.
Step 3: If danger remains, call local authorities.
Safety and humane treatment can go together.
