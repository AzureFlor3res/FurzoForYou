Stray Dog Awareness

Stay calm, avoid conflict, and choose humane responses.

Conflict risk chart

Running toward dogs (high risk)
Crowding feeding dogs (moderate risk)
Calm distance + slow movement (safe)

What NOT to do

  • Do not throw objects.
  • Do not corner or chase.
  • Do not disturb dogs with puppies.
If approached: Stand still, avoid eye contact.
Then: Back away slowly, keep hands low.
If danger continues: Call local animal authority.
Safety and kindness can work together.
