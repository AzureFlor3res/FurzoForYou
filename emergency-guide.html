Emergency Guide

Designed for fast reading in urgent moments.

Critical symptoms

  • No breathing or collapse
  • Heavy bleeding
  • Seizure
  • Poisoning signs

First response

  1. Stay calm.
  2. Call emergency clinic.
  3. Control bleeding with pressure.
  4. Transport safely.

Decision flow chart

Step 1: Breathing normal?
No: Emergency transport now.
Yes: Check pain, bleeding, toxin exposure.
Unsure: Call vet helpline immediately.
Calm action saves lives.
