Emergency Guide

Quick reading for urgent situations.

Urgent signs

  • No breathing / collapse
  • Seizure
  • Major bleeding
  • Suspected poisoning

Immediate actions

  1. Stay calm.
  2. Call emergency vet.
  3. Give first aid safely.
  4. Transport immediately.

Decision flow

1. Is breathing normal?
No: go now to emergency clinic.
Yes: assess bleeding, pain, toxin exposure.
Unsure: call vet helpline immediately.
Fast calm action matters.
