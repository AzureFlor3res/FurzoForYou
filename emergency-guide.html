Emergency Guide

Use this page for fast action when symptoms are urgent.

Emergency signs: no breathing, collapse, seizures, major bleeding, toxin exposure.
1. Stay calm and secure the area.
2. Call emergency vet and describe symptoms.
3. Give safe first aid only.
4. Transport immediately.

Quick FAQ

Should I give human pain medicine?

No. Give only medicine approved by a veterinarian.

What if I am unsure it is serious?

Call a vet helpline immediately and describe signs.

Fast and calm action can save lives.
